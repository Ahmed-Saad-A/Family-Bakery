Who we are

We have been operating for over 30 years and are proud members of the Federation of Master Bakers. From small residential gatherings to large-scale events, our family-run team handles every project with the same care and attention to detail.

Every day we experiment, learn, and improve to keep delighting our community with new favorites and timeless classics.

  • We care about the environment

    Responsible sourcing and minimal waste in every kitchen.

  • Trusted by hundreds

    Loyal clients rely on us for their most important events.

  • Social media loves us

    Thousands of glowing reviews and shared moments.

  • Always easy to work with

    Simple ordering, transparent pricing, and speedy service.

Our location

Our flagship shop sits in the heart of Los Angeles and houses our main production kitchen, tasting room, and coffee bar. Step inside to experience warm hospitality and the smell of freshly baked bread every hour of the day.

We also operate a mobile team to cater events across Southern California, bringing the Family Bakery experience directly to your guests.
